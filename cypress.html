Cypress Point Club

Cypress Point is located on the Monterey Peninsula in California. Designed by Alister MacKenzie, it is known for its dramatic coastal setting, with holes that play through dunes, forest, and along cliffs overlooking the Pacific Ocean.

The course feels natural and flowing, with holes that follow the land rather than fighting it. The routing takes players through different environments, building toward a famous stretch along the shoreline on the back nine.

Signature Stretch

The closing holes on the back nine are some of the most photographed in golf. One par 3 plays across a rocky cove to a green set on the edge of the ocean, capturing the challenge and beauty of coastal golf.
