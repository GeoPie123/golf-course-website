Welcome

This website introduces four golf courses that are widely considered among the best in the world: Augusta National, Cypress Point, Pine Valley, and St Andrews (Old Course). The goal is not to rank them, but to give a brief look at what makes each place unique.

Golf course rankings change over time, but these four names almost always appear near the top of any list. They combine history, strategy, and scenery in a way that has shaped how golfers and architects think about the game.

How to Use This Site

  • Use the navigation bar on the left to jump to any course page.
  • The Home page explains the purpose and structure of the site.
  • Each course page includes a short overview, design features, and a place for photos.

What You Will Learn

On each course page, you will find:

  • A brief background and location.
  • Key design elements or famous holes.
  • Why many golfers consider it one of the best layouts in the world.

Use this site as a quick introduction. Whether you already follow professional golf or are just curious about iconic courses, these four examples offer a good starting point.
