Welcome

This website introduces four golf courses that are widely considered among most as the best in the world: Augusta National, Cypress Point, Pine Valley, and St Andrews (Old Course). The goal of this website is to give a short preview of each course, not to rank them.

Golf course rankings change over time, but these four names are almost always consistently near the top of any list. They combine all different aspects of the game in ways that have shaped how golfers and architects think about the game.

How to Use This Site

  • Use the navigation bar on the left to jump to any course page.
  • The Home page explains the content of the website and all the courses that will be listed.
  • Each course page includes a short overview, design features, and a place for photos.

What You Will Learn

On each course page, you will find:

  • A brief background and location.
  • Key design elements or famous holes.
  • Golf course architect.
  • Difficulty using course and slope rating to calculate.
  • Why many golfers consider it one of the best layouts in the world.

Use this site as a quick introduction. Whether you already follow professional golf or are just curious about iconic courses, these four examples offer a good starting point.
